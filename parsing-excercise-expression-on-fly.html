Parsing: Evaluating Expressions on the Fly

Give yourself a pat on the back! This is the final exercise of the recursion module.

Your task is to evaluate expressions as its tokens are encountered. Fill in the details so that you have your own recursive method to evaluate the binary expression tree.

Parsing: Evaluating Expressions on the Fly - Details

Step 1: watch the video above to see the execution of an EvalTree.java solution.

In this exercise, expressions are entered in prefix notation. So adding 5 to 2 is: + 5 2 and adding 5 to the product of 2 and 3 is: + 5 * 2 3

There is no guarantee that all the strings are valid integer expressions. In fact some non-expressions will be used to test your solution.

Step 2: Open recursion/src/parsing/ExpEval.java and fill in the details of the recursive solution.
