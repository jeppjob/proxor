Your first problem to solve: Addition

Addition Task: You have two stacks of coins. How many do you have all together?

Approaching the algorithm

Question: How do I know when I’m done? Answer: You’re done when all the coins are on the first stack.

Hint #1: Know how to take a single step.

As the first stack increases by 1 the second decreases by 1 so the total is preserved.

Hint #2: Know how to be certain that each step takes you closer to being done.

Eventually, the second stack will be empty.
